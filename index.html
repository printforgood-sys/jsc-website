What We Do

Direct marketing strategy,
print & digital solutions.

From grassroots campaigns to hi-tech multichannel appeals — we build and execute the fundraising programs that grow your donor base and increase revenue.

Direct Mail Fundraising

Full-service acquisition and renewal campaigns: strategy, copy direction, segmentation, print production, Canada Post optimization, and lettershop management. Senior attention on every package.

Donor Acquisition

List sourcing, cold acquisition packages, and control testing to build your donor file. We understand Canadian list brokers, CASL compliance, and the economics of your first-gift break-even.

Data & Segmentation

Merge/purge, NCOA hygiene, RFM segmentation, suppression file management, and complex matrix work. Nili Pervin brings 15+ years of data experience to every file we touch.

Donor Retention & Upgrade

Mid-level upgrade programs, lapsed reactivation, sustainer conversion, and stewardship packages. We design the test matrix that finds your best next move — cost-effectively.

Multichannel Integration

Direct mail paired with email, QR codes, and digital landing pages. We plan the mail drop, the follow-up sequence, and the reporting that proves what worked.

Print Production & Lettershop

FSC-certified, 100% post-consumer recycled paper available. We have sourced environmentally responsible print since the 1980s. Lorie McCauley manages lettershop with 20+ years of experience.

How We Do It

A rigorous process.
Craft at every step.

When things look impossible — tight timing, impossible budgets, a campaign that needs to work — we find the path forward.

1

Audit & Strategy

We review your last three campaigns: response rates, average gift, cost-per-dollar-raised, and segmentation logic. We identify the test that would have made the biggest difference — and build the strategy around it.

2

Data & Segmentation

Your donor file is cleaned, deduped, and segmented by recency, frequency, and giving level. We suppress the donors you shouldn’t mail, upgrade the ones you should, and build a test plan with proper sample sizes.

3

Creative & Production

Appeal letters, reply devices, envelopes, and digital companion pieces. Meagan Crump’s design experience ensures every element is print-ready, on brand, and on deadline. FSC-certified and recycled paper available.

4

Print, Lettershop & Mail

Variable data lasering, collation, insertion, and Canada Post optimization — including incentive rates for charities. We manage the full production stream and distribute internationally at a fraction of conventional postal rates.

5

Results, Reporting & Next Test

After every campaign we deliver a plain-language performance report: response rate, average gift, CPDR by segment, and a recommended test for the next campaign. The most expensive test is the one you didn’t do.
